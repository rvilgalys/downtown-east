Come out for a bar crawl of six local bars and beverage producers of Durham's 'Downtown East!'

Features 2 breweries, a cidery, a meadery, a distillery, and a fantastic wine shop, Downtown East is rapidly developing into a great hotspot of beverages (and food trucks).

Collect maps at each location, and get a stamp, inital, or sticker from each location. Snap a photo of your completed map and post it to Instragram, tag each business and #DurhamEastCrawl for a chance to win a gift card from each bar!
